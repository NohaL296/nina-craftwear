    At Nina Crochet, we offer lessons for all skill levels, whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your crochet techniques. Our classes are designed to be fun, interactive, and informative, ensuring that you have a great time learning and creating.

    Available Crochet Classes

    Beginner's Crochet

    This class is perfect for those who are just starting out. Learn the basics of crochet, including how to hold the hook, make basic stitches, and create simple projects like scarves and blankets.

    Intermediate Crochet

    If you already know the basics and want to take your skills to the next level, our intermediate class will teach you how to tackle more complex stitches and patterns.

    Advanced Crochet

    For seasoned crocheters, our advanced class dives deep into intricate designs, including lacework, crochet garments, and other challenging projects.
